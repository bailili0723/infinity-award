03 — The Proposal

Goal in. Campaign out.

One story to show you what this looks like in practice.

🤖
A Chinese robot vacuum brand breaking into the US Great product, zero US brand awareness, no local creator relationships. Small team in Shenzhen. $10,000 budget.
Marketing lead types one sentence

"$10K budget, launch robot vacuum in the US, conversion focus." — that's it. No platform knowledge needed. 0-to-1, fully automated from here.

AI catches real-time US trends & generates the plan

AI detects "clean with me" and "smart home setup" are trending on US TikTok this week. Auto-generates a 14-day plan: 60% budget on creator content, 40% in-feed ads, with 3 creative angles — home tour reveals, before-and-after transformations, pet hair challenges.

AI matches 10 US home & lifestyle creators

Each ranked by historical GMV, audience-fit score, and predicted ROI. Team picks 6, clicks "Launch." AI generates creator briefs automatically, in English, with product talking points localized for the US audience.

AI Remix: creatives evolve in real time

Day 3: one creator's "pet hair challenge" video goes semi-viral. AI remixes the top clip — swaps hooks, adjusts CTAs, generates variants for different audience segments — and replaces underperformers. No manual editing, no waiting for China's morning to approve.

Full-loop automation for 14 days

AI continuously chases trending moments, reallocates budget to top creators, rotates creatives, auto-adjusts bids. Daily brief shows what changed and why. A team of 2 in Shenzhen just ran a full US creator campaign without ever touching the platform.

0 → 1 Full-Process Automation: "Goal in → Campaign out." A China-based team runs a US campaign. No local ops. No expertise gap. Just results.

06 — Product Roadmap

From reactive tools
to industry infrastructure.

Four milestones. Each one unlocks a step-change in platform capability.

🚀 DAY 30 Reactive → Proactive

Predictive AI Copilot

Trend Forecasting + Pre-emptive Fixes

The system no longer waits for performance to drop before alerting. It predicts ahead. Example: "System predicts this creative will see CPA increase 20% within 3 days due to audience fatigue. 3 replacement videos auto-generated. One-click to swap?"

Strategic value: Shift from reactive to proactive. Eliminate the "black box wasting my money" fear. AI becomes a senior media buyer who's always watching the dashboard.
⚡ DAY 60 Zero-Friction Launch

AI Campaign Builder

Goal + Budget In → Creative + Audience + Bid Out → Publish

Prompt-to-Campaign. Advertiser inputs only goal and budget. System auto-completes creative mix, audience targeting, bid strategy, and publishes — full workflow compressed from hours to minutes.

Strategic value: Head-to-head with Meta ASC on "Simpler & Faster." Break the constraints of ad groups and plan structures — let AI optimize globally across the full budget.
🎬 DAY 90 Native Creative Moat

Creator-Powered Ads

TikTok Creator Content (TTC) + AI Integration

Deep integration of AI generation with the creator ecosystem. System extracts top-performing creator assets (with authorization), applies AI remix — hook swaps, script generation, localization — to batch-produce short-video matrices with authentic creator feel.

Strategic value: Delivers "Differently & Creator-led." Solves TikTok's biggest bottleneck: high-quality short video at scale. Real creators + AI efficiency = native feel without the "obviously fake" risk, at a fraction of the cost.
🌐 DAY 360 Business Model Evolution

AI Agency as Product Mode

Standalone SaaS-grade Solution for Brands, MCNs & Agencies

Package AI capabilities into a standalone "AI Agency" product tier. Serve not just individual brands, but brand portfolios, MCN networks, and real agencies with cross-account budget allocation, full-portfolio asset management, and global strategy generation.

Strategic value: From efficiency tool to industry infrastructure. Turn competitors into partners — empower agencies with AI (enable, not replace) so they can serve more long-tail clients. Grow the total pie together.
